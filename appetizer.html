Buffalo Chicken Pastry Sliders

These Buffalo chicken pastry sliders take very little time and effort and are a great party treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 (17.3 oz package) frozen puff pastry (such as Pepperidge Farm®), thawed
  • 1 1/2 cups buffalo chicken dip,
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • hot sauce to taste (such as Frank’s® Buffalo Sauce or Frank’s® Hot Sauce)
  • 1/4 cup finely diced celery
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • blue cheese dressing for dipping (optional)

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2

Cut each pastry sheet into 6 rectangles. Stir together buffalo chicken dip and cheese in a bowl. Place a heaping dollop (about 2 tablespoons) chicken mixture in the center of each pastry square, allowing for all 4 sides of the pastry to be exposed. Set on the prepared baking sheet.

Step 3

Bake in the preheated oven until sliders are puffed and golden, 10 to 15 minutes.

Step 4

Top each pastry with a drizzle of hot sauce. Sprinkle each pastry evenly with celery and blue cheese and serve with ranch dressing if desired.

Cook's Note:

Instead of mozzarella cheese you can also use shredded Monterey Jack or Cheddar cheese.
